# Praecipe for a Writ of Ejectment

## In the Court of Common Pleas of Chester County, Pennsylvania

**To the Clerk of the said Court:**

**PRAECIPE QUOD REDDAT**

YOU are hereby commanded to issue a Writ of Ejectment, directed to the Sheriff of Chester County, in the name of the Commonwealth, at the suit of **Thomas Clarke**, plaintiff, against **Alistair Finch**, defendant, in the following form, to wit:

---

**Commonwealth of Pennsylvania**

**To the Sheriff of Chester County, Greeting:**

WE command you that you cause **Alistair Finch** to have our Writ, directed to him, in these words:

**Commonwealth of Pennsylvania**

**To the said Alistair Finch, Greeting:**

WHEREAS **Thomas Clarke**, by his attorney, hath complained unto us that you, with force and arms, have entered into one messuage, with the appurtenances, situate in the Township of West Whiteland, in the County of Chester, containing by estimation fifty acres, be the same more or less, bounded as follows: *Beginning at a white oak standing on the north side of the road leading from the Borough of West Chester to the Town of Downingtown; thence by the line of marked trees north twenty degrees east one hundred perches to a stone heap; thence south seventy degrees east eighty perches to a black oak; thence south twenty degrees west one hundred perches to the said road; thence along the road to the place of beginning*; which said messuage, with the appurtenances, the said Thomas Clarke claims to be seised of in his demesne as of fee, and that you, the said Alistair Finch, have unjustly and without title entered into the same, and do withhold the possession thereof from him, to his great damage, and against the peace of this Commonwealth.

AND THEREFORE we command you that, justly and without delay, you cause the said Alistair Finch to have the possession of the said premises, or that you shew us cause to the contrary thereof; and that you have there this Writ.

WITNESS, **John Barclay**, Esquire, President of the Court of Common Pleas of Chester County, at West Chester, this _____ day of __________, in the year of our Lord one thousand seven hundred and ninety‑five.

---

**Prayer for Relief**

The plaintiff prays:

1. That a Writ of Ejectment issue as above set forth.
2. That upon the return of the said Writ, judgment be entered for the plaintiff for the possession of the said premises.
3. That the plaintiff recover of the defendant his damages for the wrongful detention of the said land, and for the loss of use and profits thereof, together with the costs of this suit.

**Respectfully submitted,**

**Elias Whitaker**
Attorney for the Plaintiff
West Chester, Chester County, Pennsylvania
